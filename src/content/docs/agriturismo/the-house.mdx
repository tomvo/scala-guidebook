title: The house
description: Drinking water and downstairs area access.
---

**Drinking water.** The tap water is filtered and softened. You can drink it straight from the tap. In the downstairs area you'll also find cold still and sparkling water on tap.

**Downstairs area.** Open 07:30–22:00. If the door is locked during these hours, use the keypad: enter your personal code and press Yale to unlock.
