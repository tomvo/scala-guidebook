# **Good to Know**

## **❄️ Comfort & Climate**

You can use the thermostat (small panel on the right) to adjust air conditioning or heating. The round button turns it on/off.

Please keep windows closed while using the A/C and reduce intensity when you're out to save energy.

Would you like a lighter bed sheet or extra blanket? Just ask!

## **🧹 Housekeeping & Extras**

For stays over 2 nights, we offer a light clean every other day. To replace towels, please leave used ones on the floor — hanging towels won't be changed.

The bathroom includes toiletries and a hair dryer. Ask us if you need anything else.

Going to the beach? You can grab our Scala hammam towel!
