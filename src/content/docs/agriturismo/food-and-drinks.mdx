# **Food & Drinks**

## **🍷 Drinks**

In the downstairs area, help yourself to:

- **On the house:** Coffee, tea, and still or sparkling water
- **Bar tab:** Wine, craft beer, soft drinks, and juices — just note your drinks on your room's bar tab

## **🍽️ Farm Dinners**

We organise regular aperitivo evenings and farm-to-table dinners using ingredients from our garden and local producers.

We'll let you know when the next one is planned — we'd love to have you join!
