**Drinks.** In the downstairs area, help yourself to:

- Coffee, tea, still and sparkling water, on the house
- Wine, craft beer, soft drinks, and juices (noted on your room's bar tab)

**Guest fridge.** There's a fridge in the downstairs area where you can keep your own drinks or snacks. A cutting board, plates, and basic utensils are there too, if you want to put together a light bite.

**Farm-to-table dinners.** We host regular sunset farm-to-table dinners through the season. We'll let you know what's on. We'd love to have you join.
