Breakfast is served on the front terrace from 8:00 to 9:30. We prepare it to match what you feel like, and to avoid waste. Yoghurt with fruit and granola, homemade bread with jam, cheese and cold cuts, an egg cooked how you like it.

Eggs from our chickens, bread baked here, fruit and herbs from the garden, honey from our bees, and the rest from local producers we know and trust. Let us know about allergies or dietary needs (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free) at check-in.
