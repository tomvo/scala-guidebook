title: Housekeeping & extras
description: Cleaning, towels, beach towels, and bathroom.
---

**Housekeeping.** For stays of three nights or more, we do a light room clean and towel change every other day. Just let us know at breakfast if you'd like to skip or reschedule.

If you'd prefer a daily service, two options are available:

- Daily fresh towels: €5 per night
- Daily fresh towels with light room clean: €15 per night

Just let us know and we'll arrange it.

**Towels.** On cleaning days, leave used towels on the floor to have them replaced. Hanging towels won't be changed.

- **Beach towels.** Heading to the beach? Grab one of our Scala hammam towels, one per guest for your stay. Dry it on the wooden rack outside and remember your towel number.
- **Bathroom.** Toiletries and a hairdryer are provided. Ask us if you need anything else.
