# **Welcome to Agriturismo Scala**

We're so happy to have you here! Make yourself at home and enjoy everything the agriturismo has to offer.

## **🌿 Relax & Explore**

There's plenty to enjoy right here at the agriturismo:

- A cosy lounge area inside the B&B
- Front terrace
- Hammocks and seating area under the pergola
- Seats with sunset views over the hills of Recanati

Feel free to wander the grounds: explore the olive grove, fruit orchard, and kitchen garden. Our back terrace is part of our family space, but you're welcome to walk past it to explore the land!

## **🥐 Breakfast**

Breakfast is served on the front terrace from **8:00 to 9:30**.

We prepare breakfast on request to match your preferences and reduce food waste. For example: yogurt with fruit & granola, homemade bread with jam, cheese, meats and other toppings, or an egg prepared how you like it.

We use fresh ingredients from our garden and other local producers. Our eggs come from a nearby farm and are organic and free-range.

Please let us know if you have any allergies or dietary needs (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, etc.).
