# **Sustainability & Shop**

## **♻️ Our Sustainability**

We try to keep things simple, natural, and respectful of the land.

- **100% green energy** — solar panels, battery storage, heat pump
- **Sustainable materials** — tencel duvets, soft organic bed linens and towels, natural cleaning products, eco toiletries
- **Recycling & composting** — food scraps go back into the garden for next season's vegetables, fruit and flowers
- **No unnecessary packaging**
- **Organic and regenerative farming** — to protect soil life, capture water, and promote biodiversity

You help by reusing towels, being mindful with energy, and enjoying local food and our farm products. Thanks for being part of it!
