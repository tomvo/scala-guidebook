---
title: Year-round (closed July & August)
description: Macerata, Ancona, and Ascoli Piceno, monthly vintage & antiques markets outside the summer break.
---

Bring cash, most stalls don't take cards.

- **Macerata, Il Barattolo** · 2nd Sunday monthly, 9:00–20:00 · Piazza della Libertà and the surrounding *portici*. Small antiques, vintage, crafts, collectables. 2026: 10 May, 14 Jun, 13 Sep, 11 Oct, 8 Nov.
- **Ancona, Curiosando** · last Saturday + Sunday, 9:00–19:00 · Piazza Cavour (sometimes Viale della Vittoria). Skips June, July, August. 2026: 25–26 Apr, 23–24 May, 26–27 Sep, 24–25 Oct.
- **Ascoli Piceno, Mercatino Antiquario** · 3rd Saturday + Sunday monthly. Over 150 stalls spread across Piazza del Popolo, Piazza Arringo, the cloister of San Francesco, Piazza Roma and Via del Trivio. Piazza del Popolo runs 9:00–19:00 both days; other locations Saturday 15:00–19:00, Sunday 9:00–19:00. Skips July and August (and no February edition in 2026 due to Carnival). 2026: 14–15 Mar, 18–19 Apr, 16–17 May, 20–21 Jun, 19–20 Sep, 17–18 Oct, 21–22 Nov.
