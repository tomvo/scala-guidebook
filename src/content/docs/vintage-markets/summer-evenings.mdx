---
title: Summer evenings only (July & August)
description: Fermo, Porto Recanati, and San Benedetto del Tronto, big evening markets on the coast and inland.
---

- **Fermo, Mercatino del Giovedì** · every Thursday in July and August, 17:00–24:00 · Piazza del Popolo and the surrounding streets. Running since 1983, around 180 stalls. Shops stay open late and the museums open in the evening. Worth the drive.
- **Porto Recanati, Summer market** · every Sunday in July and August, 16:00–24:00 · Corso Matteotti. General summer market rather than strictly vintage.
- **Porto Recanati Vintage Market** · 25–26 July 2026, 17:00–24:00 · Lungomare Lepanto and Via 1° Maggio. The main summer vintage edition: antiques, collectables, vinyl, vintage clothing.
- **San Benedetto del Tronto, L'Antico e le Palme (summer editions)** · 26–28 June 2026 and 21–23 August 2026, 17:00–24:00 · Viale Buozzi, Corso Moretti, and the pedestrian area around Rotonda Giorgini. The biggest antiques event on this stretch of coast, over 30 years old, attracts dealers from across Italy and Europe. Furniture, jewellery, silver, ceramics, vinyl, vintage clothing.
