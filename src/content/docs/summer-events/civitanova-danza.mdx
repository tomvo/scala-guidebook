33rd edition of the Marche's main contemporary [dance festival](https://civitanovadanza.com). Eight performances across Teatro Rossini, Teatro Annibal Caro and the churches and squares of Civitanova. Highlights in 2026: Fouad Boussouf's *Näss* (3 July, national première), the Balletto di Roma's *Carnaval Vivaldi* (16 July), and **Momix with *Botanica Season 2*** (23–24 July). Only 20 minutes from us.
