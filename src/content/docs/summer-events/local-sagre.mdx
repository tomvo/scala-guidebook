Small-scale, food-focused community festivals, the real rhythm of Marche summer. Usually evening-only, in a piazza with long communal tables. Cash, €8–€15 a plate, local wine by the jug. Dates shift year to year, ask us closer to the time.

- **Sagra del Carciofo**, Montelupone, May. The local artichoke (a Slow Food presidium) in every form.
- **Brodetto Week**, Porto Recanati, early–mid June. A week of fixed-price menus built around *brodetto alla portorecanatese*, the local fish stew.
- **Disfida del Bracciale + Sagra della Polenta**, Treia, late July / early August. Renaissance ball-game in costume, followed by a polenta festival.
- **Bracieri in Festa**, Montecosaro, August. Grilled meats, long tables, live music.
- **Festa del Grappolo d'Oro**, Potenza Picena, September. Wine harvest festival in the old town.
