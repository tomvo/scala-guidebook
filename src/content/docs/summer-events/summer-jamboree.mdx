---
title: Summer Jamboree — Senigallia
description: 31 July – 9 August 2026. Europe's largest 1940s–50s American music and culture festival.
---

Europe's largest 1940s–50s American [music and culture festival](https://summerjamboree.com), and one of the largest of its kind in the world. Ten days of rock'n'roll, swing, rockabilly and boogie-woogie across the whole of Senigallia — main stages, beach parties, vintage markets, dance camps, classic car parades, burlesque, DJs from noon till dawn. Free entry to most of it. About 1h north.
