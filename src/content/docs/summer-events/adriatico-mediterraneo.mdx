Nine days of [world music, jazz, talks, theatre and film](https://adriaticomediterraneo.eu) across Ancona's most atmospheric venues, the Mole Vanvitelliana, the Roman amphitheatre, the Arco di Traiano, the Teatro delle Muse. Focus on the cultures of the Adriatic and Mediterranean: last year brought Seun Kuti, Alborosie, Anastasio. 2026 dates usually confirmed in May.
