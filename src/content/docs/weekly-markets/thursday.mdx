---

Markets here are rooted in everyday life. Most run 8:00–13:00 and stalls start packing up from 12:30, so go in the morning. Bring cash.

- **Porto Recanati** — Lungomare Lepanto (seafront) from May to September, Corso Matteotti October to May.
- **Osimo** — Piazza Boccolini, historic centre.
