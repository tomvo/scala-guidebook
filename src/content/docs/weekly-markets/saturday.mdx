---
title: Saturday markets
description: Civitanova's regional giant, Recanati's historic centre, and Macerata's smaller piazza market.
---

import { Aside } from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

- **Civitanova Marche**, Piazza XX Settembre and Corso Dalmazia. One of the biggest in the region, especially for shoes and clothing.
- **Recanati**, historic centre, around Piazza Leopardi.
- **Macerata**, 8:00–14:00, smaller, in Piazza Mazzini.

<Aside type="tip" title="Tip: lunch at the Vecchia Pescheria, Civitanova">
A few minutes from the Saturday market, in Piazza Gramsci, stands the old fish market, a Liberty-style building with dolphins carved into the roofline. The stalls around it serve that morning's catch fried in paper cones, best eaten standing up with a glass of local white.
</Aside>
