---
title: The Coast to Loreto
description: River path down to the sea, then north along the coast to Loreto and the Basilica della Santa Casa.
---

- **Distance:** approx. 22 km return (via Porto Recanati)
- **Time:** 2–3 hours return
- **Terrain:** flat cycle path, gentle climb into Loreto
- **Difficulty:** easy to moderate

Follow [The River](/biking/routes/the-river/) route to Porto Recanati, then turn north along the seafront. The coastal cycle path takes you through Scossicci and past the Chiesetta della Banderuola, a small rural church where, according to tradition, the Santa Casa rested before reaching Loreto.

From here the path climbs gently inland to Loreto. Lock the bikes and walk up the Scala Santa to the Basilica della Santa Casa for the views over the valley and the sea. Return the same way, or loop back via the quiet country roads above Porto Recanati.
