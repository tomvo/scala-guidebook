---
title: The River
description: Flat ride down to the Potenza river valley and along the cycle path — inland to the Abbazia di San Firmano, or east to Porto Recanati and the sea.
---

- **Distance:** approx. 10–13 km one way (20–26 km return)
- **Time:** 2–3 hours return
- **Terrain:** flat, paved cycle path along the river
- **Difficulty:** easy

Ride downhill from Scala to the Potenza river valley. You pick up the cycle path that follows the Fiume Potenza, flat and quiet the whole way: fields, irrigation channels, and if you are lucky, nesting herons.

**Inland, along the north bank (our favourite).** The path follows the river upstream for about 10 km through open countryside, ending near the Abbazia di San Firmano, a beautiful Romanesque-Byzantine church near Montelupone. It is peaceful, green, and feels a long way from anything. Turn around when you are ready and ride back the same way.

**East, along the south bank to the coast.** The path runs about 3 km along the river, then a left turn takes you up between farms to reach the asphalt route. Turn right towards Porto Recanati, where you reach the sea. Stop for a coffee on the lungomare before riding back.
