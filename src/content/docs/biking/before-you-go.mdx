---
title: Before you go
description: A few things to check before you ride out — battery, helmets, water, return time, road safety.
---

Three routes we love, all starting from Scala. The e-bikes handle the hills for you, so you can focus on the views. Charge is full when you set off; range is roughly 60–80 km depending on how much you use the motor.

**Battery.** Fully charged at departure. Range is 60–80 km in eco mode. For the hilltop loop, use eco on the flat and boost only on the steep climbs.

**Helmets.** They are in the bike shed. Please wear them.

**Water.** Bring a bottle. There are bars in Porto Recanati, Loreto, and Recanati, but not much in between.

**Return time.** Please let us know when you plan to be back. Half-day rides should return by 13:00 or 17:00; full-day rides by 19:00.

**Road safety.** The cycle paths are traffic-free, but some connecting roads are shared with cars. Avoid cycling along busy roads (Strada Regina, for example); crossing them is fine. On quieter roads, stay right, signal turns, and take care on blind corners.
