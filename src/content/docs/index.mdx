We're so glad you're here. This little guide is our personal pick of the places, restaurants, beaches, markets and day-trips we love most around here, the kind of things we'd tell a friend coming to visit.

## How the guide is laid out

The sidebar walks you through everything in this order:

- **[Practical things to know](/practical-things-to-know)** for the basics of your first day out: doctors, supermarkets, opening hours, dinner times.
- **[At Agriturismo Scala](/agriturismo/quick-info)** is about the house itself: rooms, breakfast, e-bikes, what's around the property.
- **[Exploring the area](/exploring/at-your-doorstep/potenza-picena)** lists places sorted by distance, from right on your doorstep to a full day out.
- **[Best of](/best-of/beach-for-swimming)** has quick picks for the things you're most likely to want: a swim, a gelato, a great view, a long lunch.
- **[Weekly markets](/weekly-markets/thursday)** and **[Vintage & antiques markets](/vintage-markets/year-round)** show what's on, where, and on which day.
- **[Summer events](/summer-events/macerata-opera-festival)** covers opera, dance, festivals and the village sagre.
- **[Restaurants, the full list](/restaurants/potenza-picena)** is every place we recommend, grouped by area.
