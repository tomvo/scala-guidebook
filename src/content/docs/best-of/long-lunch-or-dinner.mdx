---
title: Best long lunch or dinner
description: Feet-in-the-sand fish at Portonovo, seafood in Porto Potenza, or open-fire meat inland.
---

The restaurants on the beach at **Portonovo** (Marcello, Da Emilia) for fish with your feet almost in the sand. Closer to home on the coast: **Giamirma** on the Porto Potenza Picena seafront — simple, seafood, right on the water, ten minutes away. For a meat-focused feast inland, **Ristorante I Gelsi** outside Macerata — everything cooked on the open fire, in a 1700s country house setting.
