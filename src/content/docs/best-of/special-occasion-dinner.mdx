---
title: Best special-occasion dinner
description: Uliassi (three stars), or three one-star options closer to home.
---

**Uliassi** in Senigallia (three Michelin stars) if you're happy to plan weeks ahead. Closer to home: **Andreina** in Loreto, **Clandestino** on Portonovo beach, or **L'Arcade** in Porto San Giorgio (all one star).
