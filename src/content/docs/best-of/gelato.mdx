---
title: Best gelato
description: La Cremosa Cremeria Artigianale — and the honey gelato at Caffè del Teatro in Montelupone.
---

**La Cremosa Cremeria Artigianale**. Three shops in Civitanova Marche (Corso Garibaldi 225A is the main one) and the original in **Trodica di Morrovalle**, 15 minutes from us. Artisanal, 100% gluten-free, excellent vegan options. Two *coni* from Gambero Rosso. For something closer and more charming: the honey gelato at **Caffè del Teatro** in Montelupone.
