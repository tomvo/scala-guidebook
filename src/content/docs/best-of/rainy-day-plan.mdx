title: Best rainy-day plan
description: The Frasassi Caves — one of Europe's largest cave systems, entirely indoors.
---

Drive inland to the **Frasassi Caves** (about 1h15). One of Europe's largest cave systems — cool, spectacular, entirely indoors. Pair with the Tempio del Valadier. Alternatives: the underground tunnels beneath **Osimo**, or the **Buonaccorsi Museum** in Macerata.
