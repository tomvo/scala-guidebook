---
title: Best beach for swimming
description: Mezzavalle in the Portonovo bay, clear water, dramatic cliffs, and an easier alternative.
---

**Mezzavalle**, in the Portonovo bay. A 20-minute walk down from the car park keeps the crowds thin; clear water, dramatic cliffs. Take food and water. For an easier option, **Sassi Neri** in Sirolo has the same water without the hike.
