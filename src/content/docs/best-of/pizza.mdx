---
title: Best pizza
description: Proper pizza napoletana in Civitanova or Montecosaro, and wood-fired Marche cooking close to home.
---

For proper pizza napoletana, either **Donna Sofì** in Civitanova or **Il Segreto di Pulcinella** in Montecosaro Scalo. Closer to home, **La Cipolla d'Oro** on Contrada San Girio for wood-fired pizza and Marche cooking in a restored farmhouse.
