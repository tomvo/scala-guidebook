Three contenders, each a different mood:

- **Piazza Belvedere in Potenza Picena**, our own hilltop square. An early evening *passeggiata*, then dinner al fresco at **Le Scalette** or a beer and live music at **Groove**. Just five minutes from us — the easiest "best of" on the list.
- **The terrace of the basilica in Loreto** at sunset, looking south across the Conero hills to the Sibillini mountains. On a clear day you can see the whole spine of central Italy.
- **The Monte Conero clifftops** at Passo del Lupo — wild Adriatic views straight down to the sea, at their best near sunset.
