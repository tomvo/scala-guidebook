title: Weekly markets

---

Markets here are rooted in everyday life. Most run 8:00–13:00 and stalls start packing up from 12:30, so go in the morning. Bring cash.

- **Wednesday, Macerata**, 8:00–14:00, through the historic centre around Piazza Mazzini and Piazza della Libertà. The bigger of Macerata's two.
- **Thursday, Porto Recanati**, Lungomare Lepanto (seafront) from May to September, Corso Matteotti October to May.
- **Thursday, Osimo**, Piazza Boccolini, historic centre.
- **Saturday, Civitanova Marche**, Piazza XX Settembre and Corso Dalmazia. One of the biggest in the region, especially for shoes and clothing.
- **Saturday, Recanati**, historic centre, around Piazza Leopardi.
- **Saturday, Macerata**, 8:00–14:00, smaller, in Piazza Mazzini.

<Aside type="tip" title="Tip: lunch at the Vecchia Pescheria, Civitanova">
A few minutes from the Saturday market, in Piazza Gramsci, stands the old fish market, a Liberty-style building with dolphins carved into the roofline. The stalls around it serve that morning's catch fried in paper cones, best eaten standing up with a glass of local white.
</Aside>
