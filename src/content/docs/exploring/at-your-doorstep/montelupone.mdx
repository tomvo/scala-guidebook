---
title: Montelupone
description: A medieval borgo, honey capital, and the home of the artichoke.
---

A *Borgo più bello d'Italia*: medieval walls, panoramic tower, Romanesque abbey, and the small Teatro Nicola degli Angeli on the main *piazza*. Montelupone is *Città del Miele*, renowned for its honey. Stop at Caffè del Teatro on the piazza and try their honey gelato (or the local artichoke, a Slow Food presidium).
