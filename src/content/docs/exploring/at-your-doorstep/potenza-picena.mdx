title: Potenza Picena
description: Our hilltop town — piazzas, pizza, aperitivo spots, and the places on our doorstep.
---

Our hilltop town, with Teatro Bruno Mugellini, the old church of San Francesco, and a handful of cafés. Walkable, quiet, and a good place for an early-evening *passeggiata*.

- **Le Scalette** — Simple osteria close to home. Al fresco dining on the piazza in summer, with views of the Adriatic Sea and Monte Conero.
- **Osteria del Vicolo** — A slightly more refined spot, Slow Food–recommended, on Viale Cesare Battisti 1. Nice local dishes.
- **Marchigiano Reggiano** — On Piazza Matteotti. Good for aperitivo or dinner with a *tagliere*.
- **Groove Beer Bar** — Pub at the Belvedere with great sunset views, excellent at night. Occasionally live music.

**Pizza:**

- **La Cipolla d'Oro** — wood-fired pizza and Marche cooking in a restored farmhouse in Contrada San Girio.
- **Pizzeria Diego** — both a sit-down restaurant and takeaway, on the Circonvallazione Le Grazie in Potenza Picena. Good everyday option, friendly.
- **Takeaway to bring back to the agriturismo** — Pizzeria Diego (above) or **Hotel Torresi** on Contrada Strada Regina. Both five minutes away, ready in 15–20 minutes if you call ahead.

- [**Il Giardino dell'Alpaca**](https://ilgiardinodellalpaca.com) — Two-hour guided walks with an alpaca through woods and vineyards. Call or WhatsApp Roberta: +39 371 480 9442
- [**Torrenova Golf**](https://torrenovagolf.it) — Nine-hole course and driving range, 10 minutes away. Daily fee is around €30.
