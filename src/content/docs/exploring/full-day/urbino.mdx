---
title: Urbino
description: UNESCO Renaissance city, Raphael's birthplace, and the Palazzo Ducale.
---

A UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the great Renaissance cities — Raphael's birthplace, and home to the Palazzo Ducale, which holds Piero della Francesca's *Flagellation* and *Madonna of Senigallia*, and Raphael's *La Muta*. Worth the drive (about 1h45 each way). You can stop at the borgo of **Gradara** on the way back — the castle is well preserved and only a few minutes off the A14.
