---
title: Porto San Giorgio & coast south
description: A Michelin-star dinner at L'Arcade, small plates at Banco_12, and a natural wine bar on the sand.
---

About 40 min south. **Ristorante L'Arcade** (one Michelin star) for fine dining; its sister **Banco_12** in the old covered market for small plates. Further south at **Porto Sant'Elpidio**: **Controvento**, a women-owned natural wine bar on the beach with bottle service on the sand.
