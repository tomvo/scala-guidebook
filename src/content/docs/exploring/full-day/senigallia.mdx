title: Senigallia
description: The Velvet Beach and a serious food scene — Uliassi, Sepia by Niko, Vino e Cibo.
---

The *Velvet Beach*, plus a serious food scene: **Uliassi** (three Michelin stars) is one of Italy's great restaurants (tasting menus €180–€240). For something more approachable: **Sepia by Niko** (Mediterranean seafood, Sicilian–Marche flavours) or **Vino e Cibo** (small, intimate osteria near the centre, natural wines).
