---
title: Cingoli
description: Italy's Borgo dei Borghi 2026, the Balcony of Le Marche, 631 metres up.
---

Winner of Italy's *Borgo dei Borghi* award in 2026. Known as the *Balcony of Le Marche* for its sweeping views from 631 metres up, with intact medieval walls and Lago di Castreccioni just below for summer swimming. For lunch or dinner: **Taverna di Ro** in the centre, traditional recipes well executed. About 45 minutes inland.
