title: Frasassi Caves

---

One of Europe's largest [cave systems](https://frasassi.com), tucked into the hills about 1h15 inland near Genga. Cool and spectacular. Pair with the [Tempio del Valadier](https://acquafrasassi.it/it/tempio-del-valadier), a neoclassical church built into a rock gorge a short walk away.
