title: Sibillini Mountains National Park
description: Alpine peaks, clear lakes, and remote trails — great for hiking and biking.
---

Alpine peaks over 2,000m, clear lakes, and remote trails. Great for hiking and biking — we recommend the Komoot app to match hikes to your level.

**Highlights:**

- **Elcito** ("the Tibet of the Marche") and the **Canfaito** beech forest, famous for autumn foliage
- **Sarnano** — base for hikes to waterfalls and peaks; also tandem paragliding over the Sibillini
- **Lame Rosse** — striking red rock formations near Lago di Fiastra
- **Pizzo Meta** and **Lago di Pilato** for advanced hikers
- **Lago di Fiastra** — swimming, hiking, picnics
- **Il Tiglio** (Montemonaco) — one-Michelin-star, tasting menus deep in the Sibillini. Worth building a day around.
