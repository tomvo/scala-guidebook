---
title: Ancona
description: Regional capital, Italian Capital of Culture 2028, Roman arch, cathedral, and a restaurant on stilts.
---

The regional capital, named *Italian Capital of Culture 2028*, about 40 minutes up the coast. A Roman triumphal arch on the old port, the Romanesque cathedral of San Ciriaco high on its headland, a strong archaeological museum, and the green bulk of Monte Conero rising behind the city. For a romantic dinner, **La Vecchia Pesca** is a historic restaurant built on stilts over the water, seafood-focused and panoramic.
