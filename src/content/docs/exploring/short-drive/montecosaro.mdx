title: Montecosaro
description: A quiet hilltop borgo, proper pizza napoletana, and the Bracieri in Festa sagra.
---

A quiet hilltop borgo, home also to the **Bracieri in Festa** sagra in August. For pizza: **Il Segreto di Pulcinella** in Montecosaro Scalo, proper pizza napoletana, worth the short drive.
