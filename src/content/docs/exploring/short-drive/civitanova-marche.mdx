---
title: Civitanova Marche
description: Saturday market, Vecchia Pescheria fish lunches, and some of the best seafood on the coast.
---

Big Saturday market and, year-round, a cluster of casual fish-lunch spots around the Liberty-style old fish market on Piazza Gramsci (see weekly markets for the *Vecchia Pescheria* tip). For a proper dinner: **Mescola**, innovative and sophisticated, with excellent drinks, or **Donna Sofì** for pizza napoletana. Two of our own favourites: **Braceria Mei** — grilled meats done properly, and **Ristorante La Limonaia** for high-quality seafood, outside Civitanova centre.
