---
title: Macerata
description: Provincial capital, cathedral, historic university, and great trattorias.
---

Provincial capital with a Renaissance cathedral, a historic university, the intimate Teatro Lauro Rossi, and the Buonaccorsi Museum. In summer the open-air Sferisterio hosts one of Italy's great opera festivals (see summer events). Weekly markets on Wednesday and Saturday.

- **Da Silvano** — historic inn in the centre, excellent grilled meat and vegetables; also pizza.
- **Osteria dei Fiori** — small trattoria in an alley off the centre, traditional regional cooking (*vincisgrassi*).
- **Il Quinto** — down-to-earth dishes, lovely intimate setting, friendly owners.
- **Ristorante I Gelsi** — meat-focused, open-fire cooking in a 1700s country house setting just outside town.
- **Koinè Vineria** — wine bar and restaurant.
