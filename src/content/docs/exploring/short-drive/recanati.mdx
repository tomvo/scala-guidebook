title: Recanati
description: Hometown of Leopardi. Museum, the Colle dell'Infinito, and dinner in the historic centre.
---

Hometown of the poet Giacomo Leopardi. Visit the [museum](https://giacomoleopardi.it) in his birthplace and walk up to the [Colle dell'Infinito](https://fondoambiente.it/orto-sul-colle-dell-infinito-eng), the garden that inspired his most famous poem. Good for dinner: **Il Borgo Antico** in the historic centre, modernised Italian classics in a basement setting.
