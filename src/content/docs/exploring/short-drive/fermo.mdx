A blend of rich history, Renaissance architecture, and sweeping Adriatic views. Excellent museums, the ancient Roman cisterns beneath the old town, and, in July and August, the much-loved Thursday evening antiques market.

<Aside type="tip" title="Tip: museums">
Download **Musei Italiani**, the free official app from Italy's Ministry of Culture. It covers over 400 state museums, opening hours, and lets you buy tickets without booking fees. Available in English.

First Sunday of every month, entry to all state museums is free (*#DomenicalMuseo*), year-round, summer included. Expect it to be busier than usual; the app lets you reserve a slot where required.
</Aside>
