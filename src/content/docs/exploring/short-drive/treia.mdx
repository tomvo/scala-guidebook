---
title: Treia
description: Hilltop gem with noble palaces and the Disfida del Bracciale re-enactment.
---

Hilltop gem with noble palaces and sweeping views. Known for the *Disfida del Bracciale*, an annual historical re-enactment of a Renaissance ball game. For lunch or dinner: **Antica Fornace** in the historic centre, simple setup, traditional recipes well executed.
